TIRAN ISLAND SNORKELING BOAT TRIP

5 photos

Enjoy a day boat around Tiran Island and explore beautiful underwater life. W’ll stop at 3 different snorkeling with spots amazing colorful coral reefs. Enjoy open buffet lunch and soft drinks on the boat.

Description

  • We will pickup you from your hotel, transfer to the yacht marina in an air-conditioned bus. Board the boat and relax on board, enjoying stunning views of the Red Sea
  • We start sail to Tiran Island. It takes around an hour to reach Tiran island and if you are lucky you might see dolphins on the way.
  • Then, . W’ll stop at 3 different snorkeling stops in Tiran Island surrounded by fantastic coral reefs, where you can snorkel along the corals, and see hundreds of fish.
  • Have a delicious open buffet lunch with magnificent views, after that snorkeling tour is ending, you will move to the marina and back to your hotel.

Departure time & Return

Pickup: Around 08:00 (Depending on your hotel location in Sharm el sheikh).
Drop Off: Around 17:00 Pm .
Duration: 8 hours.
Tour available Everyday

Tour price

  • Adult  : 25€ per person.
  • Child  : from 5 years to 10 years: 15€
  • Infant : Under 5 years old are Free of charge.
  • You can add intro dive 10€

What to bring

Towels & swimming clothes & sun cream & copy of your passport
