Discover Scuba Diving DSD

If you want to try scuba diving, but aren’t quite ready to take the plunge into a certification course, Discover Scuba Diving is for you.

The PADI Discover Scuba Diving (DSD) is a short try-diving experience for adults and children aged +10. This course introduces you to the basics of scuba diving without certifying you as a diver. The DSD program is a half-day activity, so is ideal for those interested in trying scuba, but lacking spare time. The course has theoretical session + practical session a dive in swimming pool followed by a guided dive in one of the most beautiful reefs in the Red Sea.

Price: 55€

1 or 2 Intro dive from the boat

Imagine your first dive from the boat, A full day adventure that takes you to explore the beautiful under water life of the Red Sea. Bring your family with you to share your unique experience. Drinks, lunch, equipment, transfer included.

Price: 35€-45€

PADI Scuba Diver Course

PADI Scuba Diver Course is a subset of PADI Open Water Diver Course. If you are short on time but really want to become a diver, PADI Scuba Diver might be right for you on depth 12 meters, Certification and equipment included. Duration: 2 days.

Price : 300€

 Open Water Diver course

Get your certification with Open Water Diver Course the world’s most popular and widely recognized scuba course. It consists of three main phases : knowledge development to understand basic principles of scuba diving. Confined water dives to learn basic scuba skills. Open water dives to use your skills on depth 18 meters, Certification and equipment included. Duration: 3 days.

Price : 350€

Advanced Open Water Course

The course helps build confidence and expand your scuba skills through different adventure dives from boat or house reef. An orientation check dive or scuba review dive is mandatory before starting this course. Certification and equipment included. Duration: 2 days.

Price : 300€
